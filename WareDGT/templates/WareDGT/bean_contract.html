ECX White Pea Beans Contract

2. Grading Parameters

2.1 General Requirements

  • Good natural color
  • Free of objectionable odor
  • Free from live insects, toxic seeds, glass and metal fragments
  • Moisture content ≤ 13% by weight

2.2 Definitions

  • Moisture Content: Determined on wet-weight basis using an approved moisture meter
  • Foreign Matter: All matter other than white pea beans (including pods) that remains on or passes through a 3.57 × 19.05 mm sieve
  • Defects: Damaged beans and splits
  • Damage: Beans damaged by disease, insects, weather, or other causes
  • Insect-bored Beans: Beans bored by insects (dead insect may be present)
  • Splits: Sound beans or detached seed-coats which are three-fourths or less of a whole bean
  • Good Natural Color: Pure appearance unaffected by natural or man-made factors
  • Objectionable Odor: Unpleasant smells from weathering, contamination, mould, disease, or insects
  • Contrasting Class: Beans differing in color, size, or shape from the predominant class

3. Standard Trading Terms

  • Standard Lot Size: 50 quintals net weight (tolerance ± 4%)
  • Maximum Order Size: 20 contracts (in 50-quintal increments) = 1,000 quintals per transaction
  • Tick Size: 1 Birr minimum price movement
  • Daily Position Limit:
    • Member + all clients: 1,000 lots/day
    • Single member or client: 200 lots/day
  • Daily Price Filter: ± 5% of previous day’s close
  • Warehouse Receipt:
    • Trades only on ECX-issued receipts after grading, weighing, deposit in ECX-certified warehouse
    • Tradable for 60 days from deposit; expires upon trade execution
    • Penalty: 3.5%/day on tradable value after expiry
  • Contract Quote Basis: “Ex-Warehouse” (exclusive of taxes, fees, charges)
  • Quotation Factor: Birr per quintal
  • Trading Session: ሰኞ–አርብ (የህዝብ በዓል ቀናትን በስተቀር)

4. Standard Settlement Terms

  • Pay-in of Funds: Buyer’s account debited T+1 working day
  • Pay-out of Funds: Seller’s account credited T+1 working day
  • Weight Tolerance Adjustment: Difference between actual and contract weight adjusted at settlement
  • Exchange Transaction Fee: 0.4% of transaction value (buyer & seller)
  • Handling & Certification Fee:
    • Buyer: Birr 2.6 per bag/quintal
    • Seller: Birr 2.7 per bag/quintal
  • Warehouse Storage Charge:
    • Birr 0.16 per bag/quintal/day for first 30 days
    • Birr 0.32 per bag/quintal/day thereafter

5. Standard Delivery Terms

  • Delivery Centres: Addis Ababa, Adama, Shashemene (separate contract per location)
  • Delivery Notice: Issued T+1 after trade (notification to buyer’s Member)
  • Pick-Up Notice (PUN): Must be registered after Delivery Notice but before Trade + 5 calendar days (T+5)
  • Delivery Period: Trade + 5 calendar days (T+5) to pick up without extra charges
  • Failure to Pick Up: Penalty of 1% per day of transaction value beyond T+5
  • Extension of Delivery Period: Allowed by Exchange under force majeure
