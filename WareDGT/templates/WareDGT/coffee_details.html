ECX Coffee Reference

Coffee Grading Parameters

General Requirements

  • Moisture content ≤ 11.5% by weight.
  • At least 85% of beans remain on top of a 14 screen.

Key Definitions

  • Washed Coffee: Green coffee prepared by wet processing.
  • Unwashed Coffee: Green coffee prepared by dry processing.
  • Specialty Coffee: Distinctive coffee with full cup taste and minimal defects.
  • Commercial Coffee: Coffee not qualified as specialty.
  • Local/Domestic Coffee: Inferior quality due to impurities or prolonged storage.
  • Raw Value: Sum of points from primary/secondary defects, shape & make, color and odor.
  • Cup Quality Value: Sum of cup defect count, acidity, body and flavor.
  • Liquoring: Cup testing by professional tasters.

Washed Commercial Coffee (Raw 40% | Cup 60%)

  • Raw Value (40%)
    • Defects 20% (Primary count 10%, Secondary weight 10%)
    • Shape & Make 10%
    • Color 5%
    • Odor 5%
  • Cup Quality Value (60%)
    • Cup Cleanness, Acidity, Body, Flavor each 15%

    • Odor 10%

Grade boundaries are the same as washed coffee. Under Grade applies to coffee scoring 15–19 with less than 50% sound beans.

Specialty Assessment

  • Fragrance, Flavor, Aftertaste, Acidity, Body, Uniformity, Balance, Clean Cup, Sweetness and Overall are scored from 6.00 (Good) to 9.75 (Outstanding).
  • Q1: Preliminary Grade 1–2 and Cup ≥ 85 points.
  • Q2: Preliminary Grade 1–3 and Cup ≥ 80 points.
  • Grade 3 Specialty: Preliminary Grade 1–3 and Cup < 80 points.

Standard Trading Terms

  • Standard Lot Size: Washed 30 bags × 60 kg; Unwashed 30 bags × 85 kg.
  • Weight Tolerance: ± 4%.
  • Warehouse Receipt Basis: Only ECX-issued receipts; tradable once.
  • Receipt Expiration: 30 days from issuance; expires on trade execution.
  • Post-Expiration Penalty: 3.5% per day on current tradable value.
  • Max Order Size: 100 lots per transaction.
  • Tick Size: 1 Birr increment.
  • Daily Position Limits: Member + all clients 1000 lots/day; single client 200 lots/day.
  • Daily Price Filter: ± 5% of previous close.
  • Price Quote: Ex-Warehouse at delivery centre.
  • Quotation Factor: Birr per feresula.
  • Trading Session: ሰኞ–አርብ (የህዝብ በዓል ቀናትን በስተቀር).

Standard Settlement Terms

  • Pay-in of Funds: Buyer’s account debited T+1 working day.
  • Pay-out of Funds: Seller’s account credited T+1 working day.
  • Weight Tolerance Adjustment: Settled against contract standard weight.
  • Moisture Loss Adjustment: Seller 0.0065% per day of storage; buyer 0.0065% per day beyond T+5.
  • Exchange Transaction Fee: 0.4% of transaction value.
  • Handling & Certification Fee: Seller Birr 2.70/bag; Buyer Birr 2.10/bag.
  • Warehouse Storage Charge: Birr 0.16/bag/day (first 3 days grace).

Standard Delivery Terms

  • Delivery Centres: ECX warehouses listed in Section 1.
  • Delivery Notice: Issued T+1 after pay-in.
  • Delivery Period: Buyer must pick up within 5 calendar days (T+5).
  • Pick Up Notice: Member registers a PUN before collection.
  • Failure to Pick Up: Penalty of 1% of transaction value per day past T+5.
  • Extension: Delivery period may be extended under force majeure.
