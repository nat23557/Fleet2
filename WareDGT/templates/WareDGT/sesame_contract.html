ECX Sesame Contract (Oct 2010 v2)

2. Sesame Grading Parameters

2.1 General Requirements

  • Sesame Seeds shall have a good natural color, free of objectionable odor, free from live insects, free from visible mould and have a maximum moisture content of 10% by weight.

2.2 Definitions

  • Moisture Content: determined on a wet-weight basis using an approved moisture meter.
  • Foreign matter: anything other than sesame seeds that passes a 1.0 mm sieve (or any mineral/plant/animal matter that does not).
  • Good Natural Color: the pure, unaffected natural appearance of the grain.
  • Objectionable Odor: an unpleasant smell from weathering, chemical contamination, mould, disease or insect damage.
  • Contrasting Colour: seeds differing in colour from the predominant class.

3. Standard Trading Terms

  • Standard Lot Size: 50 quintals net weight (±4% tolerance)
  • Maximum Order Size: 20 contracts (1,000 quintals) per transaction
  • Tick Size: 1 Birr minimum price movement
  • Daily Position Limit:
    • Member + all clients: 1,000 lots
    • Member or single client: 200 lots
  • Daily Price Filter: ±6% relative to previous close
  • Warehouse Receipt: All trades must be backed by an ECX Warehouse Receipt (single use):
    1. Producer → exporter
    2. Exporter → exporter (for less-than-container re-trade)
  • Receipt Expiry: 60 days from deposit (expires on trade execution)
  • Post-Expiry Penalty: 3.5% per day on the tradable value
  • Quote Basis: “Ex-Warehouse”, exclusive of taxes/fees/charges
  • Quotation Factor: Birr per quintal
  • Trading Session: ሰኞ–አርብ (የህዝብ በዓል ቀናትን በስተቀር)

4. Standard Settlement Terms

  • Pay-in of Funds: Next business day (T+1)
  • Pay-out of Funds: Next business day (T+1)
  • Weight Tolerance Adjustment: Settled on the actual vs. contract weight difference
  • Exchange Transaction Fee: 0.4% of transaction value (buyer & seller)
  • Handling & Certification Fee:
    • Buyer: Birr 2.6 per bag/quintal
    • Seller: Birr 2.7 per bag/quintal
  • Warehouse Storage Charge:
    • First month: Birr 0.16 per bag/quintal/day
    • Second month: Birr 0.32 per bag/quintal/day

5. Standard Delivery Terms

  • Delivery Centers: Addis Ababa, Metema, Bure, Nekemte, Humera, Gondar, Asossa
  • Delivery Notice: Next working day after trade date
  • Pick-Up Notice (PUN): Issued after delivery notice, before T+5 calendar days
  • Delivery Period: Trade date + 5 calendar days (T+5)
  • Failure to Pick Up: 1% per day of trade value penalty after T+5
  • Extension: Permitted by ECX for force majeure or approved written request
